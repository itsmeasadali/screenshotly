# About Asad Ali

Asad Ali is a seasoned Full-Stack Developer and the Founder & CEO of [ZTabs](https://ztabs.co/), a digital services company specializing in innovative software solutions. With over 8 years of experience in the tech industry, Asad has built a reputation for creating scalable, high-performance web applications and APIs.

## Expertise & Experience

Asad's technical expertise spans across modern web technologies, with particular strength in:

- **API Development**: Designed and optimized APIs serving millions of requests
- **Performance Engineering**: Specialized in reducing load times and improving scalability
- **SaaS Architecture**: Built multi-tenant applications with complex business logic
- **Modern Web Stack**: Expert in Next.js, React, Node.js, TypeScript, and PostgreSQL

## Professional Background

As the founder of ZTabs, Asad has led the development of numerous successful projects including:

- **Agiled**: A comprehensive project management platform with 10k+ active users
- **Billed**: Smart invoicing platform processing $2M+ in payments
- **SchedulingKit**: Advanced booking system handling 1M+ appointments
